      How to Use

      Customize your Focus Mode experience by managing your blacklist and whitelist:

      • Blacklist: Add websites you want to block during focus sessions.
      • Whitelist: Add exceptions - websites you want to access even during focus mode.

      Enter domain names without 'http://' or 'www.' (e.g., 'facebook.com').
